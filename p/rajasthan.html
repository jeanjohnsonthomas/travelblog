Exploring The Desert Wonders and Fortress Paradise: Royal Rajasthan

31 Oct, 2024

Jaipur -> Jaisalmer -> Thar Desert -> Udaipur

Hey travelers,

In the previous blog I talked about my experiences in Mumbai, And the next destination after visiting Mumbai was Rajasthan. I hope you are ready to hear about the magnificent forts, enchanting places, mesmerizing desert and the travel experiences. I also fulfilled my wish of visiting the golden desert and experiencing the smooth silky sand by myself, which brought me a profound sense of happiness. Get ready to teleport with me to the land where history comes alive and the enchanting nature of the places fills your soul with wonder and joy.

DAY 1 : THE BEGINNING

Me and my friends, after extensive planning, decided to visit rajasthan. For this we booked flights and Mumbai was just a one day intermediary stop for us. We caught the vistara flight to Mumbai at 6:00 am. I was very excited for this flight because I had only traveled once in flight, that too when I was 5 years old. I keep constantly asking doubts about luggage, the procedure to follow at airport, etc out of excitement of doing something new. The boarding went smoothly and we waited for our flight. I was assigned the window seat, and was eagerly waiting for the lift off. The flight took off pretty smoothly. As the plane ascended into the sky, I could see the gradual change in the landscape below, the cityscape shrinking into miniature buildings, and the vastness of the sky engulfing us.

Amidst the splendor of Mumbai's skyline, I couldn't help but reflect on the contrasts we had witnessed during our brief stay. The luxury skyscrapers and sprawling slum settlements painted a striking picture of the city's socio-economic diversity. It served as a reminder of the resilience and resourcefulness of its residents, navigating life's challenges within the towering structures of progress. As night descended upon Mumbai, we boarded our 10 pm flight, bidding farewell to the city that had captured our hearts in unexpected ways. Touching down in Jaipur on the morning of January 27th, we carried with us memories of Mumbai's warmth, chaos, and spirit, ready to embrace the next chapter of our journey through Rajasthan's enchanting landscapes.
